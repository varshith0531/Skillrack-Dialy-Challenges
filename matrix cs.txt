List Comprehension - Exercise 001 Complete the program using List Comprehension so that the program accepts a M*N matrix and prints the sum of elements in the 4 corners. - M is the number of rows - N is the number of columns. Example Input/Output 1: Input: 3 3 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 9 Output: 20 Explanation: The sum of the corner elements is = 1+3+7+9 = 20 Example Input/Output 2: Input: 5 4 14 4 6 1 4 17 11 4 10 5 13 8 14 11 2 10 14 10 9 14 Output: 43 Explanation: The sum of the corner elements is = 14+14+14+1 = 43
